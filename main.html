Kerala is a state located in the southwestern region of India. It is known for its beautiful backwaters, palm-lined beaches, lush greenery, and rich cultural heritage. Kerala has a population of approximately 33 million people and its capital city is Thiruvananthapuram. The official language of Kerala is Malayalam.
